## Stem

## Code Snippet (optional)

## Answer Key (Correct Answer) 
Validators that are selected randomly to mine, or validate a block, are trading energy for cryptocurrency.

## Distractors
### 1.
A group or individual would have to own 51% of the staked cryptocurrency to execute an attack and alter the blockchain.

### 2.
Coin owners who stake coins become validators and are randomly selected to mine new blocks.

### 3.
Many different mechanisms can be used for validating blocks, depending on the architecture of the blockchain.

## Common errors, misconceptions, or irrelevant information: 
Common misconceptions: That the roles of “miners” and “validators” are always defined within the scope of Proof of Work.
